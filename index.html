Proposition - part 1

A waffle stool

The thinking behind our proposition was to make something that does not require any glue, nails or other objects besides the wood we mill. We sought inspiration from here. and decided to make a waffle stool that could be press fit.

Proposition - part 2

A LEGO brick

The thinking behind our proposition was to make something we all love and cherish from our childhood, a LEGO brick. This seemed lika a project that we could take on, especially givern our limited experience with toolpath creation in Fusion 360. The design of the LEGO brick was a simplified version of the ones you commonly find from the creators of LEGO.
